About me!

José Pedro Oliveira, b. 1993 Guimarães – Portugal, is a Biomedical Engineer, enthusiast about technology, innovation, and science. Graduated from the University of Coimbra in 2016, holds a Master in biomedical engineering, with specialization in Clinical Informatics and Bioinformatics.

Curious and self-taught was involved in jeKnowledge, a junior startup of University of Coimbra, where played as innovation project manager, participating in the development process of different technological solutions for clients, and managed and organized several entrepreneurship events.

Last years co-developed and implemented Face2Ceph, cooperated as a researcher in various projects in eye field, and collaborated as a research fellow in the H2020 project GrowMeUp, working in the digital audio and speech processing, having published a high-performance toolbox for real-time multi-language speech recognition and direction of arrival computation for robotic platforms.

Currently focus his attention on movement disorders, playing as Full-Stack Software Developer at Kinetikos.
