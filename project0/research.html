Research

Back in 2014, while studying, I got involved in a volunteer summer internship on acoustic signal acquisition and processing in the vision field, hosted by the Electrical Engineering and Computers Department of the University of Coimbra. That experience got so much importance to me that lead me to develop my master thesis in both software and eye field and recently it was published by Elsevier in one of the most important journals of Biomedical Engineering. This pathway conduced me into a research fellowship on the same department, that aimed to develop a companion and assistance robot for the independent elderly. My goal was to develop and improve the human-like interface through speech (capable of recognizing multi-language elderly speech as well as synthesize audio capable of being understood by end users)!
